Facts About Cats

Cats have a flexible skeleton and no collarbone, which allows them to fit through any space their head can fit through. They also have a specialized reflective layer in their eyes called the tapetum lucidum, which helps them see in low light.

A group of cats is called a "clowder." Studies show that cats can make over 100 different sounds, whereas dogs can only make about 10!
